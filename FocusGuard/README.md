# FocusGuard
> A screen time monitoring app designed to help parents and teachers ensure kids stay focused on their educational work.

## The Problem
Parents and teachers often struggle to determine if children are actively engaging with their assignments or simply getting distracted by games on their devices. Furthermore, many parents find it difficult to check, track, and manage their children's screen time effectively. This lack of visibility makes it challenging to promote healthy digital habits and enforce focused study times.

## The Solution
FocusGuard is a web app that helps parents and educators seamlessly monitor and manage children's screen time. The app provides a clear dashboard to see active application usage, allowing adults to quickly verify if kids are doing their schoolwork or getting distracted. By bringing these insights into one easy-to-use place, FocusGuard empowers parents and teachers to guide children towards better focus and a healthier relationship with technology.

## Features
- **Real-Time Activity Dashboard**: View exactly which applications or websites kids are spending their time on.
- **Screen Time Analytics**: Track daily and weekly usage trends to understand how much time is spent on productive tasks versus entertainment.
- **Distraction Alerts**: Quickly see when educational time is interrupted by gaming or off-task behavior.
